About Me

My path into AI started with curiosity about how machines could understand language and images the way people do. That curiosity turned into a structured pursuit: a Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence, an AI Engineer track with Microsoft through the Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative, internships spanning banking and research, and a growing portfolio of projects across NLP, Computer Vision, and Reinforcement Learning.

Today, I am focused on building AI systems that are not just accurate, but explainable and trustworthy — work that comes through clearly in my graduation research on credibility assessment and contradiction detection in criminal interrogations, combining NLP, Knowledge Graphs, and Graph Neural Networks.
